About Me

Hi, I am Rohini Khan—a curious UI/UX researcher and designer, a third-year B.Tech student, and an AI enthusiast who enjoys crafting thoughtful digital experiences. I love exploring the intersection of human-centered design and technology, where ideas evolve into intuitive and impactful interfaces.

I am a PwC Women Leadership Development Program (WLDP) trainee, and I strongly believe in a disciplined lifestyle—one that fuels consistency, focus, and long-term growth. To me, great design mirrors discipline: structured, intentional, and continuously refined.

I have contributed as a Graphic Designer for initiatives like Driveblaze and the Google Gemini Student Community, and I enjoy experimenting with tools such as Figma, Canva, and Wix to bring ideas to life. I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for front-end development, and I specialize in UI/UX Design. I also work with React to build modern, responsive web applications.

When I am not designing or coding, I am learning, improving, and quietly building meaningful solutions that make a difference.
